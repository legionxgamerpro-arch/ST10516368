Website Choice 

Name: Zano Market

Organisation Description: 

Zano marketplace is a small, rented space which is community driven. Platform serves local vendors and entrepreneurs allowing them to sell products and highlight their talents, we also target customers seeking locally produced products by increasing their accessibility. Our mission is to empower local businesses and strengthen community connections one order at a time.



We are one of few businesses that focus on promoting economic growth and encourages sustainable entrepreneurship by providing local vendors and entrepreneurs with a cost-effective platform to sell products and grow their businesses responsibly, which sets us apart from our competitors.



We offer a highly diverse product range supplied to us by local vendors, entrepreneurs and artisans including fresh produce, clothing and Potter and Accessories which are all made with natural ingredients and recycled materials packed in durable eco-friendly packaging.



Reasons for Choosing the Organisation: 

The reason I created this community driven marketplace is because it promotes economic growth and supports sustainable entrepreneurship by providing its local vendors and entrepreneurs with a cost-effective platform to sell their products and a chance to grow their businesses responsible. The business makes it possible for community members to have ease of access to diverse local products.

 



Website Goals and Objectives:

Goals and Objectives



•	Enhance user experience by improving the site speed and navigation to make shopping easier for customers.

•	Increase Sales Revenue by improving product pages, images and descriptions.

•	Generating leads by adding signup forms like newsletters and account registration.

•	Enhancing brand visibility and Building trust by improving search engine rankings and including testimonials to build credibility.



Key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success:

•	Conversion rate: This will be the percentage of viewers on the website who complete a desired action such as buying a product or filling out a form.

•	Traffic Sources: This will identify where viewers originate e.g. search engines, social media and ads. This will help to measure Search Engine Optimization (SEO) success and ad performance.

•	Average time on page: Measuring viewers engagement by tracking their time spent on the site or specific pages.

•	Bounce Rate: This the percentage of viewers who leave the website after only viewing one page, a high rate may indicate poor user experience or irrelevant content.

•	Page load time: This is the speed at which a page loads which is critical for user experience and SEO rankings.

•	Return on Investment (ROI): This will be the overall profitability of the website in relation to its cost.

Layout and Design Approach

•	Navigation structure : The website has a well organised header section and navigation bar that allows users to easily to browse and access other web pages.

•	Product Display : Products are displayed using a clean grid layout.

•	Responsive Layout : The website can be view in different screen sizes and provides a consistent experience for users on mobile, tablet and desktop.

•	Forms : Fully functional forms allowing users to input data and send.



User Experience Considerations

•	Easy navigation : Clear navigation bar with links and footer section with links making it easier for users to navigate to one website after another.

•	Product Information: Products have descriptions , prices and ratings to help customers make informed decisions. 

•	User-friendly forms : contact and registration forms are labelled and easy to complete.

•	Page load times : Page is optimised to reduce load times and improves browsing experience for users.
